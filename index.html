Who We Are

A Foundation Built on Compassion

Section 8 Registered NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating lasting, positive change across communities in India. Founded on September 23, 2020, by Govind Shukla, the foundation was born from a belief that meaningful transformation happens when minds unite with purpose.

We work at the intersection of education, livelihoods, environment, and social welfare — running six active project streams that touch every stage of human life, from childhood to professional growth.

Our approach is grassroots and participatory: we train, engage, and empower communities rather than simply serving them, building resilience that outlasts our programs.

Our Work

Six Projects, One Mission

🍱
Community Service

Project SEVA

Provides food, clothing, and essential daily support to underprivileged communities and families living in need across India.

50,000+ beneficiaries supported

📚
Education

Project BACHPANSHALA

Opens doors to quality learning for underprivileged children through digital literacy, educational resources, and mentorship.

🐾
Animal Welfare

Project JEEV

Feeds and cares for stray animals, promoting compassion toward all living beings and raising awareness about animal rights.

🌸
Women Empowerment

Project UDAAN

Empowers women through skill development workshops, entrepreneurship support, financial literacy, and awareness programs.

🌱
Environment

Project PRAKRITI

Drives environmental sustainability through plantation drives, conservation activities, and community awareness campaigns.

20,000+ saplings planted

💼
Skill Development

Project VIKAS

Enhances employability through structured internships, training programs, and career development opportunities for youth.

30,000+ interns participated
